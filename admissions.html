Admissions

We welcome families who value academic excellence, character development, and a nurturing school community.

How to Apply

Our admissions process is simple and personal.

1

Schedule a Tour

Visit our campus, meet our teachers, and see our classrooms in action.

2

Submit an Application

Complete our online application form with your child's information and school records.

3

Student Assessment

Your child will participate in a brief, age-appropriate assessment.

4

Acceptance & Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete enrollment paperwork. Welcome to Woodward Academy!
