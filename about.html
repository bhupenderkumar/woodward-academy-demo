Our Mission & History

Founded in 1905, Woodward Academy has a long history of providing a high-quality education to students in Atlanta. Our school has a strong tradition of academic excellence, with a focus on preparing students for success in college and beyond.

At Woodward Academy, we believe that every student learns differently and at their own pace. Our teaching approach is student-centered, with a focus on project-based learning and hands-on activities that engage students and promote deeper understanding.

In addition to our academic programs, Woodward Academy offers a wide range of extracurricular activities and clubs that help our students develop their interests and talents. From sports teams to music programs, we encourage our students to explore their passions and develop their character.
