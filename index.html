Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for students who turn 4 years old by September 1. Our experienced teachers use play-based learning to develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a nurturing environment.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 5th Grade. We focus on building a strong foundation in reading, writing, and mathematics, while also introducing students to the arts, physical education, and character development.

📖

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students from 6th to 12th Grade. We offer a challenging and supportive learning environment that prepares students for success in college and beyond.
