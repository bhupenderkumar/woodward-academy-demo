Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for students who turn 4 years old by September 1. Our experienced teachers use play-based learning to develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a nurturing environment.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 5th Grade. We focus on building a strong foundation in reading, writing, and mathematics, while also introducing students to the arts, physical education, and character development.

📖

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students from 6th to 12th Grade. We offer a challenging and supportive learning environment that prepares students for success in college and beyond.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program provides students with hands-on experience in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We offer courses in coding, robotics, and engineering, as well as opportunities for students to participate in science fairs and competitions.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program offers students a wide range of opportunities to explore their creativity. We offer courses in music, theater, dance, and visual arts, as well as opportunities for students to participate in performances and exhibitions.

🏈

Athletics

Our Athletics program provides students with opportunities to develop their physical skills and teamwork through sports and physical education. We offer a range of sports teams and programs for students of all skill levels.
